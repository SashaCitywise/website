Bloodborne is a 2015 action role playing video game set in the fictional city of Yharnam. It was developed by Fromsoftware and was published by Sony Computer Entertainment, It can only be played on PS4

Yharnam is a mostly abandoned Gothic, Victorian like city which vasts from big tall buildings to decrepit woods. The protagonist is a nameless charcater only known as 'The Good Hunter' or 'The Hunter' who is hunting the beasts that plague the city. The protagonist is trying to find the source of the plague that is turning everybody to beasts, little do they know it was the blood all along. The game has a total of 17 bosses (with only 9 being mandatory), 21 Chalice Dungeon bosses and 5 in the DLC you can buy titled 'The Old Hunters'.

This website is dedicated to discussing the 2015 game bloodborne and the Best bosses design wise, NPC's, Overall Gameplay and lastly the overall difficulty of the game

The game has various locations you will discover as you play. The first area is Central Yharnam, Other areas include the Hunter's Dream which is essentially the games version of a HUB.

Bloodborne has a massive development team. Big names include te overall creater by Hidetaka Miyazaki. It's fantastic, compelling and awe-striking 80 minute music score was composed by a mix of Japanse and Western composers. However it has names in it like Tsukasa Saitoh, Yuka Kitamura, Nobuyoshi Suzuki, Ryan Amon, Cris Velasco and Micheal Wandmacher. It has a 65 piece orchestra and a 32 peson choir.
